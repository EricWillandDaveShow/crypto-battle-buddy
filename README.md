# Crypto Battle Buddy

A trading-discipline mentor that helps users follow the plans they create for themselves and resist emotional decision-making.

> Crypto Battle Buddy is not a trading platform, exchange, signal service, or financial-advice application.

## Purpose

Crypto Battle Buddy helps users define a deliberate plan before emotion enters the decision. It supports accountability to that plan while leaving every trading decision and action under the user's control.

## Core workflow

- Select and manage crypto assets
- Define BUY and SELL threshold tiers
- Monitor fresh CoinGecko market prices
- Identify IDLE, TRACKING, ACTION, and COMPLETE states
- Require ARM before execution or marking an opportunity missed
- Record the consequence of action or inaction
- Reinforce current-cycle and lifetime discipline

## Core doctrine

- The user creates the plan.
- CBB does not recommend trades.
- Only fresh, successful market polling may be presented as current.
- Cached or stale market information is never presented as live.
- Recovery is accountability, not prescriptive trading advice.

## Technology

- Flutter and Dart
- Android
- CoinGecko market-price feed
- Locally persisted user plans and discipline records
