## Promotional Text

## Description
Crypto Battle Buddy helps you interpret crypto market conditions using your personal budget and risk preferences, presenting clear, human-readable guidance designed to support disciplined decision-making.

The app does not execute trades, does not connect to exchanges for trading, and does not access wallets for signing or transaction approval. All actions are completed externally on platforms of your choice, fully under your control.

Crypto Battle Buddy focuses on awareness and restraint rather than automation or hype. It is designed for users who want to understand what the market is doing and how it aligns with their personal approach, without handing control to software.

### Key Features
- Plain-English market condition summaries
- Planning posture modes (Chill / Balanced / YOLO)
- Optional asset tracking and allocation planning
- Budget-aware planning to support disciplined behavior
- Clean, one-glance status messaging

Disclaimer: Crypto Battle Buddy provides informational insights only and does not provide financial advice.
